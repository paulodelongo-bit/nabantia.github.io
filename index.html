About

European Portuguese taught with clarity, structure, and care.

Nabantia was founded in Tomar to offer thoughtful, structured lessons in European Portuguese — the language as it is spoken, written, and understood in Portugal.

Behind every lesson is Paulo, a qualified teacher with academic training in Portuguese and Portuguese Language Teaching, and experience working with international learners.
