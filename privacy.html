Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nabantia collects and uses personal information submitted through this website.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Nabantia is a European Portuguese teaching project operated by Paulo, based in Tomar, Portugal.

For privacy-related questions, please use the contact form available on the main website.

2. What information we collect

When you use the contact form, we may collect the following information:

  • your name;
  • your email address;
  • your current level in Portuguese;
  • the message you choose to send.

3. How we use your information

Your information is used only to reply to your message, understand your learning needs, and discuss possible Portuguese lessons.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not use it for advertising purposes.

4. Contact form provider

The contact form on this website is processed through Formspree, a third-party form service. Formspree receives the information submitted through the form so that your message can be delivered.

5. Cookies and tracking

This website does not intentionally use analytics, advertising cookies, or marketing tracking tools.

The website may load external services necessary for its basic operation, such as Google Fonts and the Formspree contact form service.

6. How long we keep your information

Messages are kept only for as long as necessary to reply to your request and manage any resulting communication about lessons.

7. Your rights

You may ask to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have sent through this website.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
